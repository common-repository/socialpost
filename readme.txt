== Description ==

**Social Media Auto Publish Plugin - SocialPost will automatically share, auto-post, re-publish, re-post blogs posts on social media: auto-post to Facebook, Twitter, Google+, LinkedIn, Instagram, XING, Reddit, Pinterest, Flickr, Medium, Tumblr, Torial, Diigo and Bloglovin.**

Social Media Auto Publish Plugin - SocialPost is a social media auto-publishing plugin for your WordPress website. You can automatically post your blog posts to any social media like Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, Google+(Google Plus), Blogger, Tumblr, Flickr, ok.ru, LiveJournal, DreamWidth, Flipboard, Instagram, Telegram, Line, Delicious, Diigo, Instapaper, Stumbleupon, Pinterest, Plurk, YouTube, Scoop.It, XING etc from now on.

Our Technology is designed to auto post content from your blog or website to social media, making communication through social media easy and pleasant.

It is your helping hand for engaging with your social audience and keep your content alive. SocialPost is flexible and lets you auto-publish new posts and also the older ones. You can also auto-share your blog posts from WordPress post or page or product in a single click once you authorize your social media accounts. Now you can reach the most audience and tell all your friends, readers and followers about your new post or page or product.

== Installation ==

[Install SocialPost from our site](http://wpeacock.com/) by typing in your site address after login or registration.

Alternatively, install SocialPost via the plugin directory, or by uploading the files manually to your server. After activating SocialPost, create your account or connect to enable the SocialPost features.

Install the SocialPost - Social Media Auto Publish Plugin by following these steps
1. Download the latest version of the SocialPost Plugin.
2. In your WordPress Administration Panels, click on Add New option under Plugins from the menu.
3. Click on upload at the top.
4. Browse the location and select the SocialPost and click install now.
5. To turn the Social Media Auto Publish Plugin - SocialPost Plugin on, click Activate.
6. Check your WordPress blog to see if the Plugin is working.

Alternatively, you can also follow the following steps to install the plugin using FTP.
1. Download the latest version of the SocialPost Plugin to your computer from here.
2. With an FTP program, access the server of your site.
3. Upload (copy) the Plugin file(s) or folder to the /wp-content/plugins folder.
4. In your WordPress Administration Panels, click on Plugins from the menu.
5. You should see SocialPost Plugin listed. If not, with your FTP program, check the folder to see if it is installed. If it isn't, upload the file(s) again. If it is, delete the files and upload them again.
6. To turn the SocialPost Plugin on, click Activate.

or follow these steps to install SociaoPost from your own WordPress website
1. Go to Plugins and click on add new plugin.
2. Search for SocialPost.
3. After SocialPost - Social Media Auto Publish Plugin click on install and active the plugin.
4. Now you are ready to use SocialPost

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

1. How do I add Social Media accounts?
after installing the plugin go to connects menu from the main menu and log in by using your social media accounts like facebook, twitter, LInkedIn, google+, etc.,

2. Can I use SocialPost - Social Media Auto Publish Plugin just for one Social Media account or does it require all networks to be set?
Yes! If you want, you can use the plugin for just one Social Media account.

3. My site not publishing posts on the LinkedIn feed?
In LinkedIn, you won't see your posts on your feed. you can check your posts on your account activity or you can check on any other feed of your mutual accounts.


== How it works? (Technical Process) ==

SocialPost - Social Media Auto Publish Plugin will connect to social media using connections option under socialpost main menu.
After the successful connection with social media, by using our sidebar widget you can post your blog posts from your website to social media.

Our plugin will use our server [SocialPost-server] for social media connections.
